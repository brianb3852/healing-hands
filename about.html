About


I have been working as a Licensed Massage Practitioner in Washington State since 2008. I began my journey with massage in the Bay Area working with corporate clients at their jobsite. Once I became licensed in Washington, I began working with chiropractors in treatment settings as well as worked in spa environments. Communication is important and welcome at any point during the session. This allows me to tailor your session to you specifically.
