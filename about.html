About


Ive been working as a massage practitioner for over 15 years. When I was a child, my parents would often times massage my head or my belly when I wasn't feeling well. As an adult in college and living away from home, I realized how important touch had been to my healing process. After receiving my BA, I began working for an airline and witnessed, as well as experienced, work injuries that seemed debilitating. I have always been interested in anatomy and how our bodies work so, during a period of layoffs, I took the opportunity to learn about massage.

After receiving my Massage Certification in San Francisco, I began working with corporate clients providing relaxation and sports massage in their fitness facility at work. I also worked alongside an Acupuncturist with her patients to help with their treatment goals. In 2009 I became licensed as a Massage Practitioner here in Washington State. Since then, I have worked alongside Chiropractors, Physical Therapists as well as in spa environments. Each massage is tailored to you specifically so I invite as much communication as you like in order to provide you the best results.
